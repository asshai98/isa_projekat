About

Play Dungeons & Dragons with friends across the world on the award-winning Roll20 virtual tabletop. It's free-to-use, browser based, and features a full suite of tools to quickly build characters, roll dice, and run any campaign.

This app provides a rich toolset including a character builder, having your own custom character collection, profile editing tools and so on. Explore full digital conversions of official D&D content, including ready-to-play adventures, drag-and-drop monsters, integrated rulebooks, and more. Sign up for your free account at Roll20.net!
